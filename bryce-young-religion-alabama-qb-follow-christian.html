Does QB Follow Christian Faith?

April 2024 · 3 minute read

Bryce Young religion: the Alabama Crimson Tide quarterback is a devoted follower of Christianity.

Born Bryce Christopher Young, Bryce Young is an American football quarterback. He currently plays for the University of Alabama’s men’s football team.

The Alabama Crimson Tide competes in the Football Bowl Subdivision of the NCAA and the Western Division of the Southeastern Conference.

The 2022 Sugar Bowl MVP also holds the record for most passing yards in a single game by an Alabama quarterback and is also the winner of the 2021 Heisman Trophy.

With his athletic prowess, the Pennsylvania native has garnered significant fame in the league. Thus, in today’s article, let’s learn more about Bryce Young’s personal life, including his religion, family background, parents, and siblings.

Bryce Young Religion: Does Alabama Crimson Tide QB Follow Christian Faith?

The Alabama Crimson Tide quarterback Bryce Young is a devoted follower of Christianity.

Raised in a family of faith, he is also grounded by his relationship with God. In an interview with Sports Spectrum, the Heisman-winning footballer once revealed that he works to be the best version of himself that he can be and to embody Christ in all that he does.

The 21-year-old footballer further added regardless of how someone else would view him and whatever award, what people might say (positive or negative), at the end of the day, the model himself after the Lord is what motivates him the most.

Since signing with Alabama as a five-star quarterback out of Mater Dei High School in California, Bryce Young has incorporated his faith into his public persona.

His Instagram and Twitter bios mention “follower of Christ.” Furthermore,  during his Heisman Trophy acceptance speech, he first thanked God and said that without his Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ, he couldn’t be there as all things are possible through him.

Also, in his podcast, The Bryce Young Podcast, the Alabama quarterback said he spoke from the heart during his acceptance speech.

Bryce Young Parents, Family And Ethnicity

The Alabama Crimson Tide quarterback was born on 25 July 2001 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. He is the only son of  Craig and Julie Young.

Bryce Young’s parents have been married for 27 years. The quarterback’s family has always been the backbone of his career. His family has moved their entire lives to let him play his final two high school seasons at Mater Dei, a national powerhouse.

They were in Bryce’s college commitment video. Bryce’s mother and Father are active on Instagram. They frequently share his accomplishments.

Bryce Young’s Father, a consultant at Young Counsuting Group, LLC, shared some snaps from his graduation day. In the post, Craig said that his son’s graduation day was the proudest day of his and his wife’s life.

The 2022 Alabama graduate passed for 312 yards and five touchdowns in a game that other top NFL prospects might have skipped, leading his team to defeat Kansas State in the Sugar Bowl on Saturday, 31 December 2022.

As per the reports, Bryce Young’s five-TD performance paced Alabama Crimson Tide to win Sugar Bowl.
