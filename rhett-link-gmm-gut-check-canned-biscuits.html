Gut Check! Rhett &amp; Link Find the the Most Crumbelievable Canned Biscuits

August 2024 · 3 minute read

On today’s episode of Good Mythical Morning, Rhett & Link try canned biscuits—sussing out if Pillsbury deserves its famed reputation. In case Rhett & Link start to go into a biscuit haze, they have some hot herbal tea on hand to revive their palates. Disclaimer: Link’s ratings were low throughout because he is generally disappointed with biscuits that come from a can.

Pillsbury Grands! Flaky Layers Butter Tastin’ Biscuits

It’s official: Rhett & Link prefer flaky biscuits. It’s not good to be a flaky person, but it is good to be a flaky biscuit. But this fourth place ranking is big news!! To Link’s surprise, two non-Pillsbury options ultimately beat this Pillsbury Grands! biscuit in the final round. Link said the butter taste is better in the Great Value Butter Flavor Flaky Jumbo Biscuits and the Annie’s Organic Flaky Biscuits. Rhett & Link still think this biscuit is a nice option.

(Rhett: 75, Link: 53)

Great Value Butter Flavor Flaky Jumbo Biscuits

Rhett said, “It’s like a real-deal biscuit.” And adding a compliment, Link said, “The butter in this one tastes very real.” However, both Rhett & Link agreed that the top two were clear. So I guess this option could actually be butter, lolz.

Pillsbury Grands! Flaky Layers Buttermilk Biscuits

The anticipation! It’s getting hot in here!After Rhett first tasted the Grands! Flaky Layers Buttermilk Biscuits, he said, “I got a little crispy bite, which is influencing me.” Ultimately, though, there was room for improvement. When he compared the top four biscuits in the final round, Rhett noted that the flakiness didn’t quite come through with this option. The flaky branding wasn’t that accurate.

Annie’s Organic Flaky Biscuits

AND DRUM ROLL, PLEASE!! Annie’s Organic Flaky Biscuits snatched the win. Link, who is generally disappointed by canned biscuits, said this option DIDN’T TASTE CANNED. Put that on a T-shirt, Annie! Rhett agreed that this Annie’s biscuit tasted like a real biscuit. Rhett said, “It’s weird that it is that good.” 

Rhett’s Favorite: Annie’s Organic Flaky Biscuits

Link’s Favorite: Immaculate Baking Organic Flaky Biscuits

The Worst: Kroger Buttermilk Biscuits & Trader Joe’s Organic Biscuits
