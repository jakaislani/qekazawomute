Netflix Reportedly Saying Goodbye To Lucha Underground

August 2024 · 1 minute read

Wrestling fans who have been relying on Netflix to get their Lucha Underground fix, will apparently have to find another avenue to watch the program. According to Lucha Blog, the show will be pulled from U.S. Netflix users after November 15th.

Lucha Underground Season 1 & 2 will no longer be available on Netflix US after 11/15. pic.twitter.com/lo4B89xq1i

— luchablog (@luchablog) October 20, 2018

Netflix had been streaming Lucha Underground’s first two seasons, while the company finishes up its fourth season. Lucha Underground signed a deal with Netflix in March of 2017, which was then hailed as a ‘game changer‘ for the wrestling company. Despite the deal, Lucha Underground third season never even made its way to the streaming network. Ryan Satin of Pro Wrestling Sheet reports that there have been no talks of Lucha Underground being picked up for a fifth season.
