Harper was scheduled to compete in the Little Miss pageant, which took place Tuesday night.

Harper was taken by A-I Ambulance of Altamont from the accident site on Sunday shortly after 1 pm to the helipad at HSHS St. Anthony’s Memorial Hospital in Effingham.

According to the authorities, ARCH Air Medical then airlifted her to St. Louis Children’s Hospital, where she passed away on Wednesday night.

The Effingham County Sheriff, Paul Kuhns, issued a statement on Thursday afternoon: “We appreciate the public’s assistance and willingness to help in this investigation, and our deepest condolences go out to the Finn family and all of those involved.”
