    Cranberry Almond Cheese Ball

    Get ready to impress with this unbelievably delicious Cranberry Almond Cheese Ball - made with just FIVE ingredients! This easy recipe is great for entertaining and parties!

    Course Appetizer Cuisine American Keyword cheese ball, cranberry almond cheese ball Prep Time 5 minutes minutes Total Time 5 minutes minutes Servings 8 servings Calories 287kcal Author Trish - Mom On Timeout

    Ingredients

    • 8 oz cream cheese 1 package - I used light, softened
    • 4 oz sharp white cheddar cheese grated (I grate the cheese on the smaller opening)
    • 2/3 cup dried cranberries I used 50% less sugar cranberries
    • 3 green onions thinly sliced
    • 6 oz Blue Diamond Almonds 1 container - Smokehouse flavor

    Instructions

    • Combine the first four ingredients in a medium-size bowl, stirring until well combined.

    • Spoon contents onto a plate and use plastic wrap to form into a ball (or football).

    • Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

    • Cover the entire cheese ball with almonds. You may have some leftover.

    • Refrigerate until ready to serve.

    • Top with strips of cheese for the football laces if desired.

    • Serve with assorted crackers or bread.
